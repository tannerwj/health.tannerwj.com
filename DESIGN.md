# health.tannerwj.com Design Notes

## Current Status

The final visual direction is intentionally undecided. This foundation should support a later comparison of two or three distinct homepage treatments using the same representative data.

No treatment has been selected in this slice.

## Design Constraints

- Mobile-first is mandatory; the site will often be opened from X bios, DMs, and phone conversations.
- The wordmark is `health.tannerwj.com`.
- Do not add a tagline.
- The site should feel like a personal field guide: calm, distinctive, readable, and fast to scan.
- Avoid SaaS landing-page patterns, generic marketing copy, and heavy legal or medical framing.
- Peptides are one peer section in a broader health site. The calculator can be prominent later without making peptides dominate the site.
- Content pages should ship no client-side JavaScript by default.
- The future calculator page is the only planned substantial client island.
- Affiliate links should be compact and contextual, with mobile rendering treated as primary.
- Use a persistent footer disclosure: "This is what I do, not a protocol for you. Some links are affiliate links -- they don't change what I recommend."

## Deferred Visual Decisions

- Typography system.
- Color palette.
- Homepage composition.
- Section page density and grouping.
- Social card art direction.
- Calculator UI treatment.

## Foundation Slice Guidance

Until the visual bake-off happens, pages should stay plain and functional. The goal is to prove the content and data model, not to create a polished design language.

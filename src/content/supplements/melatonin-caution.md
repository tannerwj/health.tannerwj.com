In the [Huberman Lab conversation](https://www.hubermanlab.com/episode/dr-matthew-walker-the-science-and-practice-of-perfecting-your-sleep), Matthew Walker describes melatonin as a signal of circadian timing: it helps communicate that night is approaching, rather than acting as a blanket insomnia fix.

He also notes that evidence and applicability differ across people and contexts. That calls for caution around treating it as a universal sleep aid.

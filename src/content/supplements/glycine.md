Huberman's [sleep toolkit](https://www.hubermanlab.com/newsletter/toolkit-for-sleep) includes glycine among additional, optional sleep supplements. It is presented after behavioral sleep tools, not as a foundation.

Claims about changing sleep stages should be treated cautiously. The source does not make a nightly stage score a reliable way to judge an individual response.

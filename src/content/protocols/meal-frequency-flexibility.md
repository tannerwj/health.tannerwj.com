In this [Layne Norton discussion](https://biolayne.com/podcasts/dr-layne-norton-podcast/things-ive-changed-my-mind-on-episode-5/), meal frequency is framed as flexible once calories and protein are working for the person.

Frequent eating is not metabolism magic, and fewer meals are not inherently better. Distribution can still be contextual around training, appetite, and daily routine.

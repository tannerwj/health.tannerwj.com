A [randomized resistance-training study](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4594298/) found that regular cold-water immersion immediately after lifting attenuated some longer-term hypertrophy and strength adaptations. That result concerns a repeated post-session practice, not every brief cold exposure.

FoundMyFitness' [training conversation](https://www.foundmyfitness.com/episodes/how-to-train) provides the practical context: timing and the goal of the session matter. Immediate symptom relief can be valuable, but it is not automatically aligned with maximizing muscle gain.

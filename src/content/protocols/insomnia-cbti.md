The [Matthew Walker Podcast's insomnia guide](https://themattwalkerpodcast.buzzsprout.com/1821163/episodes/18530776-121-a-practical-guide-to-insomnia) distinguishes persistent insomnia from the normal occasional bad night.

For persistent insomnia, CBT-I is presented as the first-line behavioral approach. A source note cannot assess an individual sleep problem, so ongoing symptoms belong in appropriate clinical care.

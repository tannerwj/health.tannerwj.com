FoundMyFitness' [topic overview](https://www.foundmyfitness.com/topics/time-restricted-eating) and BioLayne's [meal-timing review](https://biolayne.com/reps/issue-5/can-timing-your-meals-enhance-fat-loss/) describe time-restricted eating as a structure that can support consistency or circadian alignment for some people.

It has not been shown to be universally superior to calorie-matched diets. The useful question is whether the schedule fits the person and the broader nutrition pattern.

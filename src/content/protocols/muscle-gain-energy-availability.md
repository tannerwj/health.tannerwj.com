Layne Norton's [muscle-building nutrition discussion](https://biolayne.com/podcasts/dr-layne-norton-podcast/keys-to-building-muscle-part-2-nutrition-episode-18/) treats training and adequate energy as connected parts of a muscle-gain context.

Sustained energy deficits can limit gain, while a very large surplus is not automatically better. The appropriate approach depends on the person, training, and goals rather than a fixed public target.

Peter Attia's [AMA on ApoB](https://peterattiamd.com/ama43/) describes ApoB as a way to view the number of atherogenic lipoprotein particles, rather than only the cholesterol carried inside them.

The supporting outcome literature is largely observational, including this [JACC cohort analysis](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31642874/). A marker can refine risk context, but it does not supply a personal treatment target or replace clinical interpretation.

Layne Norton's [fat-source article](https://biolayne.com/articles/nutrition/fat-facts-fat-source-matters/) argues against reducing dietary fat to a single number without considering the foods supplying it.

Energy balance remains important, but the balance of unsaturated and saturated sources can still matter in an overall dietary pattern. This is food-pattern context, not a personalized prescription.

This [review of CGM use without diabetes](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10658694/) suggests that continuous readings can be useful for noticing repeated patterns, while also describing gaps in the evidence for general wellness use.

A single spike is not a diagnosis or a complete metabolic story. Standard laboratory measures and clinician evaluation remain important when there is a health concern.

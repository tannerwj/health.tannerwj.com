Matthew Walker's [sleep-cycle post](https://x.com/sleepdiplomat/status/2036412542176670092) treats the universal 90-minute cycle as an oversimplification. Cycle length can vary across people and across the same person's nights.

The practical point is not to plan sleep around exact 90-minute blocks or assume that waking outside one invalidates the night. Sleep architecture is more variable than that rule suggests.

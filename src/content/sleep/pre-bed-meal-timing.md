Rhonda Patrick's [meal-timing post](https://x.com/foundmyfitness/status/2025967196644495503) describes her preference for finishing food roughly three hours before bed. The useful experiment is whether moving a large meal earlier changes comfort or sleep for the individual.

Three hours is her template, not a hard rule. Work schedules, reflux, training, glucose management, total intake, and hunger can all change what timing makes sense.

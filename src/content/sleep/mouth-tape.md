I use mouth tape. I don't treat it as a fix for suspected sleep apnea or blocked nasal breathing. Andrew Huberman's [public post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/andrew-huberman_mouth-breathing-during-sleep-is-not-just-activity-6962803089330372608-i3f7) and FoundMyFitness' [Michael Grandner episode](https://www.foundmyfitness.com/episodes/michael-grandner) are outside context for this note.

Matthew Walker's [2025 discussion](https://themattwalkerpodcast.buzzsprout.com/1821163/episodes/17536544-102-mouth-taping) says the hype exceeds the evidence. He advises against it with congestion or suspected, moderate, or severe apnea; it is not a treatment for those conditions.

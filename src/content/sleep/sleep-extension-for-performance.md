The [Perform Podcast discussion](https://www.performpodcast.com/episodes/how-to-enhance-performance-with-better-sleep) describes sleep extension and sleep banking as possible tools around demanding training blocks, competition, or travel.

That is performance context, not a universal athlete target. The appropriate amount and timing still depend on the person, the demand, and the broader sleep picture.

Huberman Lab's [sleep guidance](https://www.hubermanlab.com/newsletter/improve-your-sleep) and FoundMyFitness' [alcohol and sleep discussion](https://www.foundmyfitness.com/episodes/alcohol-and-other-factors-that-negatively-affect-sleep) both separate sedation from high-quality sleep.

The durable idea is that alcohol can make falling asleep feel easier while still worsening sleep architecture and next-day recovery.

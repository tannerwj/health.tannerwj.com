Peter Attia's [exercise framework](https://peterattiamd.com/exercising-for-longevity/) treats fitness as several capacities instead of a single workout type.

The durable idea is to keep strength, sustainable aerobic work, higher-intensity work, and movement capacity all visible when designing a week.
